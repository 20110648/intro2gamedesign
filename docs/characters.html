Financial Advisor: Shrewd and pragmatic, Solveig ensures resources are well-managed. Wears fine but understated clothing, with ledgers always in hand.

City Planner: Calm, detail-oriented architect with a passion for symmetry and efficiency. Helps lay out infrastructure to support rapid growth. Wears simple, practical clothing with tools hanging from a belt.

Health Advisor: Warm and nurturing, with a sharp mind for medicinal and scholarly pursuits. Adorned in a flowing cloak with herbal pouches and a small tome strapped to his hip.

Religion Advisor: A fierce advocate for balance between progress and nature. Dresses in earthy tones, with a necklace made of stones and carved wood. He carries a large staff with rune inscriptions.

Raids Advisor: Hot-headed yet loyal warrior who thrives on battle. Advocates for aggressive strategies against rivals. Sporting braided hair and an axe slung across his back, he is every inch the archetypal Viking warrior.

Public Safety Advisor: A no-nonsense former soldier turned peacekeeper. Keeps order within the settlement and mediates conflicts. Broad-shouldered, wearing a leather breastplate and always carrying her sword.

Utilities Advisor: Soft-spoken but firm, Harald manages water, food, and other essential resources. Clad in durable workwear, he is practical and hands-on.

Transportation Advisor: Adventurous and innovative, always looking for ways to improve trade routes and exploration. Wears a sea-weathered coat and a compass around her neck.
