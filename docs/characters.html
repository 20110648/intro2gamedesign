Financial Advisor: This character helps manage the village's finances and ensures economic stability.

City Planner: This character is responsible for planning and developing the village's infrastructure.

Health Advisor: This character oversees the well-being of the villagers, ensuring access to remedies, treatments, and preventative care.

Religion Advisor: This character guides the spiritual practices of the community, maintaining harmony with the gods and sacred traditions.

Raids Advisor: This character plans and executes strategic raids, ensuring successful resource acquisition and minimal losses.

Public Safety Advisor: This character ensures the protection of the village through defense planning and enforcement of communal rules.

Utilities Advisor: This character manages essential services like water, firewood, and waste, ensuring the village functions smoothly.

Transportation Advisor: This character coordinates the movement of people and goods, maintaining pathways, carts, and longships for efficient travel.
